Welcome to my Final Project!

This is Bitmoji Royale! An action oriented game in which you can play against the computer or your friends.

To play, hit the start button on the Title menu, once on the next section of the game, choose your difficulty. Difficulties for this game range from easy, hard, and impossible. To move your character in the game, use the WASD keys against the CPU. In multiplayer, use the W,A,S,D keys to control Denno and the up, down, left , and right arrow keys within the game to control Jordan. To shoot with Denno, use either the C key or the one key to fire. To shoot with Jordan, use the period mark key to fire. Enjoy!
